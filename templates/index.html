HubSpot Auditors

Choose an audit, paste your own HubSpot private app access token on the next page, and review the results directly in the browser.

How to get your HubSpot token

  1. Log into the HubSpot account you want to audit.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Open Integrations -> Private Apps, or Development -> Legacy apps if that is what your account shows.
  4. Create a new private app, or open an existing one.
  5. Enable the scopes you need for the audit you want to run.
  6. Copy the private app access token. It will start with pat-.
  7. Open either the lists or workflow auditor and paste the token into the page.

Required scopes

  • Lists Auditor: crm.lists.read
  • Workflow Auditor: automation, crm.schemas.contacts.read, crm.schemas.companies.read, crm.schemas.deals.read, crm.schemas.tickets.read
